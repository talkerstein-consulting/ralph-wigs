Our Story

The Hands Behind
Every Ralph

For seven years, Ralph Toledano trained under master Italian wig-maker Joseph Papino — building wigs for the stages of the Paris Opera. He has spent more than 46 years at the bench since, and the house has made wigs the same way since 1966.

Every Ralph is hand-made in Italy from European virgin Remy hair, on a mesh cap hand-knotted in Japan — the world's best at it. You buy straight from the atelier, never a retailer, never a markup, with a one-year warranty, always.
