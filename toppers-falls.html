Questions

Good to Know

What's the difference between a topper and a fall?

A topper adds coverage and volume at the crown/part; a fall adds length and body lower down.

Will it blend with my hair?

Yes — we color-match to your shade so it's seamless.

Is a topper good for thinning hair?

Yes — it's one of the most natural ways to cover thinning at the part.

Can I style it?

Yes — it's real European hair; heat-style and part it as you like.
