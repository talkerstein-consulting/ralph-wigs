How It's Made

Three Levels of Craft

Every Ralph is made from European, Eastern-European or South-American hair — about half the cost of any wig is the hair itself. How the hair is tied to the cap is what sets the three tiers apart.

01

Hand-Tied

Every strand knotted by hand. The most natural movement, the lightest feel, the most undetectable part — the top of the collection.

02

Hand & Machine

Half hand-tied, half machine-wefted — the balance of natural look and value that most women choose for every day.

03

Machine-Made

Expertly machine-wefted for strength at the most accessible price — full, beautiful, and built to last.
