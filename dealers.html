Two Ways to Partner

Stock It, or Refer It

Carry Ralph the way that fits your business — hold inventory and keep the margin, or simply send your client to the atelier and earn on every piece. Many partners do both.

Path One

Stock & Resell

Buy atelier-direct at trade pricing, fit and sell to your clients yourself, and keep the markup. Best for salons and machers who want wigs on hand and full control of the sale.

  • Trade pricing on approval
  • You own the client & the margin
  • Restock as you grow, backed by the house
Path Two

Refer & Earn

No inventory, no risk. Send your client to Ralph — in Aventura or on tour — and earn a commission on what they buy. Best for stylists who'd rather not hold stock.

  • Zero upfront cost
  • Ralph fits & fulfills; you're paid per sale
  • Your client, your introduction, always credited
