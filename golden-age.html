Questions

Good to Know

Are these wigs suitable for thinning hair?

Yes — it's one of the most common reasons women come to Ralph. A wig restores full, natural hair with no daily styling, and a topper adds volume over your own hair where it's thinned.

Can it be matched to my natural grey or silver?

Of course. Greys, silvers and salt-and-pepper are blended by hand so the colour reads as your own. Keep your shade or soften it — matched in person.

Will it be comfortable to wear all day?

That's the point. Light, breathable caps on Japanese-engineered mesh — it sits gently and stays put from morning to night.

Is it a lot to look after?

Far less than your own hair. A brush, an occasional wash, and it holds its shape. Ralph handles deeper refreshes and restyling for you.

I'm nervous it will look obvious.

It won't. Made of European virgin hair, matched to your colouring and cut to your face — done right, the only person who knows is you.
