# Ralph Wigs — Image Direction (canonical)
*Approved direction · supersedes the "absolute coverage / no-smile" rules in Brand Book v1 · 2026-07-01*

**The one-line brief for the image person:**
> Ralph imagery should feel like a modern Jewish woman wearing the most natural, beautiful wig of her life. She is tznius, current, feminine, warm, and approachable. The hair is the hero. The styling should be modest but not severe. She can smile. She can look at camera. She should look attractive but never seductive. Mix warm buyer-facing portraits with close-up proof shots, atelier craft, and real client-style social images. Avoid turtleneck-only, faceless-only, overly abstract, Queen-of-England, mannequin, AI-looking, or cold luxury images.

---

## 1 · Homepage hero
Real woman in a Ralph wig, soft warm light, facing camera or slightly turned, soft smile / calm pleasant expression. Modern frum wardrobe — 3/4-sleeve blouse, fine knit, cardigan, blazer, or simple modest dress (NOT turtleneck-only).
**Avoid:** downward-gaze-only, full mystery, no smile, extreme high neck, Queen-of-England styling, oversized shapeless clothes, seductive pose.

## 2 · Product proof shots (must be REAL photography — not AI)
Per major wig/look: front · 45° · side profile · back (length) · hairline close-up · part close-up · movement video · daylight colour · indoor warm-light colour. Clean, close enough to see quality, not over-retouched. Buyer judges hairline/shine/density/movement closely.

## 3 · "Who Ralph Is For" (unique image per segment — no reuse)
- **Sheitels** — modern Jewish woman, current modest outfit, soft feminine face, everyday sheitel.
- **Medical** — gentle, private, warm; soft cardigan/blouse; safe & dignified (no dramatic sadness).
- **Golden Age** — polished older woman, soft fullness, graceful, current but age-appropriate (not matronly/stiff).
- **Beauty** — more glam but tasteful; volume/colour/length; feminine confidence (not seductive).
- **Film & Theatre** — editorial/craft; Ralph adjusting, production prep, wig on real head; dramatic but classy.
- **Toppers & Falls** — clear before/after volume, crown coverage, fall length, side/back.

## 4 · Factory-direct / atelier (make "straight from the source" VISIBLE)
Ralph fitting a wig on a client · cutting/styling around the face · hands knotting/working hair · hair laid on atelier table · colour-matching · tools/combs/pins/bundles close-up · client in mirror during fitting · Ralph examining hairline/part · before/after refresh. Mood: warm, expert, private, old-world craft.

## 5 · "Worn in the Wild" / Instagram social proof
Vertical 4:5 or 9:16. Real clients / client-style, modern modest outfits, natural smile, real-life settings, clean background, hair visible & flattering. No black borders, no square crop, no fake-polished stock vibe.
Examples: walking outside with hair movement · at a simcha (modest dress, soft smile) · home mirror after styling · everyday polished outfit · kallah/mother appointment moment.

## 6 · Tour / pop-up
Private fitting moment at pop-up · Ralph with client · elegant temporary showroom · woman trying on wig in mirror · beautifully styled racks/tables · calm appointment atmosphere (not trade-show chaos) · community relevance (Flatbush/Lakewood/Monsey/Boston) without looking cheap. Mood: "an exclusive private fitting came to my city."

## 7 · Dealer page (trade/professional, not consumer-lifestyle)
Ralph with salon owner/stylist · trade box/packaging · consultation at salon chair · dealer inspecting hair · Ralph explaining product · premium display case/rack · hair-quality close-up · warm professional conversation. Mood: "I can confidently offer this to my clients."

## 8 · Two lanes (the balance IS the brand)
- **Editorial Ralph lane:** profile · back of head · hands in hair · quiet/mysterious · atelier craft · high-end luxury.
- **Buyer-conversion lane:** warm face · soft smile · gentle direct eye contact · current modest wardrobe · feminine & relatable · real-woman energy.
Too much of the first = cold. Too much of the second = generic. **Use both.**

## 9 · Wardrobe
**Use:** 3/4 sleeves · long sleeves · modern blouses · refined knits · soft cardigans · blazers · midi skirts · simple modest dresses · polished everyday frum style. Palette: neutral, ivory, navy, taupe, soft gold, espresso, black.
**Avoid:** default turtlenecks · stiff high-neck-everything · shapeless oversized · Queen-of-England · loud prints · cheap satin · visible logos · bodycon/tight · sleeveless · low neckline · seductive styling.

## 10 · Colour/image mood
Do NOT go all-gold unless **Michal approves** the brand shift (stakeholder decision, not a design assumption). Safer: Navy = trust/heritage/authority · Gold/champagne = luxury accent · Ivory = softness/background · Black/charcoal = readability/seriousness.
