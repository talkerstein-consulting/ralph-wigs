The Craft

Made the Way a
Sheitel Should Be

Every Ralph sheitel is hand-made in Italy from European virgin Remy hair — cuticle intact, all one direction, so it doesn't tangle or frizz the way processed hair does.

Questions

Good to Know

What hair are Ralph sheitels made from?

European virgin Remy hair — cuticle intact, all one direction — hand-made in Italy. It's the same hair Ralph has worked with for over 46 years.

How long does a sheitel last?

Cared for properly, it's built to be worn daily for years — and every Ralph carries a one-year warranty.

Can you match my own hair?

Yes. Bring a photo or a small sample to your fitting and we match color, length, and texture.

Is there a discount for kallahs?

Yes. Brides — and their mother and mother-in-law — receive $200 off. Mention it when you book.

Can I pay over time?

Yes. Ask about our payment plan: a deposit, then spread over three months.

How do I get one?

Book a private appointment in Aventura, or catch the next pop-up expo in New York, Italy, Paris, or Miami.
