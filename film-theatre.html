The Lineage

From the Paris Opera
to Your Production

Ralph Toledano trained for seven years under master Italian wig-maker Joseph Papino, dressing the stages of the Paris Opera. That training shows in every piece we build for camera and stage.

Every piece is hand-made in Italy from European virgin Remy hair on a hand-knotted cap — period-accurate, camera-ready, and built to take a beating night after night.
